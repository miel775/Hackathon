1. The Singularity: At the very center of a black hole lies the singularity, a point where matter is crushed to infinite density, and the laws of physics as we know them break down.

2. The Event Horizon: This is the "point of no return." It is the boundary around a black hole beyond which nothing, not even light, has enough energy to escape the immense gravitational pull.
